Privacy Policy

We're serious about your privacy—no tricks, no hidden catches, just straightforward respect for your data.
At Free Fire Item Search, your privacy is not just a feature—it's a promise. We believe everyone deserves a safe, anonymous, and enjoyable experience. That's why we built this site with privacy at its heart, not as an afterthought.

Our Human Approach to Privacy

We're players too, and we know what it's like to feel wary online. On this site, you are not a product. We don't track you, we don't sell your info, and we don't bombard you with ads. We simply want you to enjoy searching for Free Fire items, worry free.

This Privacy Policy explains—in plain language—what we do (and don't do) with your information.

What Data Do We Collect?

  • No personal info required: We don’t ask for your name, email, or any account signup.
  • No secret tracking: We don’t use cookies, pixels, or hidden scripts to monitor your activity.
  • Anonymous usage: Our servers may log basic, non-identifiable info (like general browser type or country) for site improvement only—never for profiling or ads.

In short: Your searches are your business. We don’t know who you are, and we like it that way!

How Do We Use Data?

Any data collected (like how often an item is searched) is strictly used to keep the site running smoothly, fix bugs, or make the experience better for all users. We never sell, rent, or share this information with advertisers—or anyone else.

If you reach out via email or GitHub, your contact info is used only to respond to you, not for marketing or spam.

Cookies & Analytics? Nope.

This site is built to be as privacy-friendly as possible. That means:

  • No cookies: We don’t save anything on your device, period.
  • No analytics services: We don’t use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar trackers.

If we ever add optional features that require storing data (like a favorites list), you’ll have full control and clear choices.

Open-Source and Transparent

Our code is public and anyone can inspect it. We believe in open-source not just for features, but for trust. If you’re curious or concerned, check out the code for yourself on GitHub.

Your Rights & Choices

  • You can use the site without giving us a single piece of personal information.
  • If you contact us, you can ask to have your message deleted at any time.
  • We will always notify you before changing our privacy approach. No sneaky updates.

For Kids and Parents

Free Fire Item Search is safe for all ages. We don’t knowingly collect information from anyone, including children. If you’re a parent and ever have a concern, just reach out and we’ll help.

Policy Changes

If we ever update this Privacy Policy, we’ll post a clear notice here and (if the change is big) let our users know in advance. We want you to always feel in control and in the know.

Contact Us

Still have questions? Want to double-check something? Or just want to say hi? You can reach the developer directly via GitHub @shozon-roy.
We’re always happy to chat—your trust means everything to us.
